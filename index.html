About me

IBM

After graduating from P-TECH in 2015, Rad immediately went to work at IBM and enrolled in Baruch College. For the past four years, he has been juggling the demands of work and school. He started his IBM career as an analyst, extracting insights from marketing data. He was soon looking to expand his technical skill set and became a user experience designer. In June 2015, Rad graduated from Baruch with a Bachelor's Degree in public affairs. Long term, Rad wants to devote his career to exploring how public/private partnerships can benefit mankind.
